About Me

I am Rodney Quarcoo. I am a documentary photographer and photo educator based in Ghana. I specialize in development photography and visual storytelling, helping international organizations, NGOs, aid agencies and foundations tell stories about their organization, their mission and the impact of their work. As a photo educator I teach various levels of photography and filmmaking to corporate groups and at a private university in Ghana. I am a certified Canon Trainer.

- Rodney Quarcoo
